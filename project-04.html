Poster Series

Eye-catching designs for cultural festival promotion

Completed: November 2022
Print Design

Design Challenge

The client needed a series of posters that would work both as large format prints (A1 size) and smaller handouts (A4 size) to promote their annual cultural festival. The designs needed to be visually striking from a distance while maintaining clarity of information up close.

Each poster in the series had to maintain brand consistency while having unique elements to represent different festival events.
