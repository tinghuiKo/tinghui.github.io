MOGU Logo Design

Creating a memorable visual identity for a modern lifestyle brand

Completed: March 2023
Brand Identity

Brand Concept

MOGU is a contemporary lifestyle brand focused on sustainable living and mindful consumption. The logo needed to communicate simplicity, elegance, and environmental consciousness while maintaining versatility across various applications.

Design Process

The design process began with extensive research into the brand's values and target audience. After creating mood boards and exploring various directions, we settled on a minimalist approach that combines geometric shapes with organic flow.

The final logo features custom typography with subtle rounded corners to convey approachability, paired with an abstract mark that suggests both growth and stability.
